About Me

I’m Ruth Kitty Kemuma, a data engineer passionate about transforming raw data into powerful tools for insight, impact, and equity. With a strong foundation in SQL, Python, Qlik, I specialize in designing scalable data solutions that support smarter decisions and real-world problem-solving. What sets me apart is my adaptability—whether it’s navigating complex technical challenges, working across diverse teams, or shifting between roles in education and tech, I bring a flexible, resilient mindset to every project. Coming from a background where education was often viewed as a luxury, I’m deeply committed to making learning accessible and impactful. That’s why I actively volunteer through teaching, share knowledge in my community, and work on projects that blend data with purpose. Through my role in data engineering, I’m able to live out my passion: building systems that empower people, especially in underserved communities, to reach their full potential through education and technology.
